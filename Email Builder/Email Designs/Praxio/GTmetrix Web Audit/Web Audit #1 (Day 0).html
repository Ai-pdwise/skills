We built this tool so business owners can see what's happening under the hood before spending another dollar on ads. Quick rundown of what you're looking at:

Load time is how long visitors wait before your page shows up. Over 3 seconds and you lose the majority of them before they see a thing.

Your performance score runs from 0 to 100. It grades how your site handles images, code, and server response. Anything below 50 means the site is working against you.

Then there's Largest Contentful Paint, which is how long it takes for the main content on your page to appear. Google wants this under 2.5 seconds.

And Total Blocking Time measures how long the page freezes before someone can tap, scroll, or interact. When this number is high, visitors feel like the page is broken.

These scores directly affect whether someone who clicks your ad stays or leaves.
