Three things we see in nearly every report. You can check all of them on your own site in about five minutes.

1. Check where your site is hosted.

A lot of performance problems start at the server level. If your site is on cheap shared hosting, it's competing with hundreds of other websites for speed. Your ads send traffic. The server puts them in a queue.

A surprising number of business owners don't actually know where their site is hosted. Or they set it up years ago and haven't thought about it since.

2. Check your images.

Oversized images are the single most common reason pages load slowly. If your homepage has photos over 500KB each, they're dragging everything down. This is also one of the easiest things to fix.

3. If you're on WordPress, check your plugins.

Every plugin adds weight. Some add a lot. Twenty or more plugins with half of them sitting inactive can still slow things down in the background.
